About Us

Mirich Economic And Social Research Center

At Mirich Economic & Social Research Center, we are committed to fostering positive change through rigorous economic and social research, supported by our state-of-the-art library and computer facilities.

At MESRC, we believe that knowledge has the power to transform lives, empower communities, and build a more equitable world. In a world of complexity and uncertainty, rigorous research provides clarity, insight, and evidence-based solutions to the most pressing economic and social challenges. Our commitment to excellence in research is matched only by our dedication to making a meaningful impact in the communities we serve. Through collaboration, innovation, and a shared commitment to positive change, we strive to unlock the potential of research to drive sustainable development and social progress.

As stewards of knowledge, we recognize the responsibility to translate research into action, working hand-in-hand with policymakers, practitioners, and stakeholders to effect real change.

Simon Chol Martin

Founder & CEO

Faq

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of research does Mirich Research Center specialize in?
Mirich Research Center specializes in a wide range of research areas, including but not limited to economic development, social policy, sustainable development, gender equality, governance, and public health.
How can I collaborate with Mirich Research Center on research projects or initiatives?
We welcome collaboration with individuals, organizations, and institutions interested in partnering with us on research projects, joint initiatives, and knowledge-sharing activities. Please reach out to us through our contact page to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
Does Mirich Research Center organize events or workshops for the public?
Yes, we regularly organize events, workshops, seminars, and conferences on topics relevant to our research areas. These events provide opportunities for dialogue, learning, and collaboration among researchers, policymakers, practitioners, and the public. Please check our blog page for upcoming events and registration details.
How does Mirich Research Center ensure the quality and integrity of its research?
Mirich Researh Center upholds the highest standards of academic rigor, integrity, and ethical conduct in all its research activities. Our research methodologies undergo rigorous peer review and scrutiny to ensure accuracy, reliability, and relevance.
